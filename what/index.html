Four guarantees no one else gives you

This is what separates a serious booking platform from a directory listing. Every gig, every booker, every artist — same protection, same standard, same rules.

Your payments are guaranteed

Bookers fund the gig up front. Artists get paid on time, every time. No more chasing invoices three months later. No more "the cheque is in the mail." When the gig is done, the money moves — and StageLink365 stands behind it.

You’re covered by our insurance — on every gig

Every booking on StageLink365 is covered. Public liability, equipment, performer cover — baked in, not an add-on. Venues stop asking artists to email a certificate. Artists stop paying for a separate annual policy. The platform carries it.

Attorney-approved contracts — on every booking

Every gig is processed through a contract drafted by entertainment lawyers. Clear deposit terms, cancellation rules, technical riders, force-majeure language — the same agreement that protects an A-list venue protects a wedding-band gig. No more 1-page Word docs. No more handshake-and-pray.

A roster you can actually trust

ID-verified. References-verified. Background-checked. Every profile on StageLink365 carries the trust badges it’s earned — nothing more, nothing less. The bookers who hire for serious money see who they’re dealing with. The artists who do serious work get the credit for it.
